Banana bread

Moist loaf, great for breakfast.
Steps
1Mash the ripe bananas in a bowl.
2Mix in the eggs and sugar.
3Fold in the flour.
4Bake for 60 minutes at 180°C.
Ingredients
Flour200 g
Bananas3 pcs
Sugar100 g
Eggs2 pcs
